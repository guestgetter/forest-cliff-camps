Common questions

What if my child has never been to overnight camp?
Most campers are first-timers. Cabin leaders are trained to help with homesickness and nerves. By Tuesday, most kids have forgotten they were nervous.
What does the $30 deposit actually get me?
It reserves your child's spot and gives you priority session selection. Fully refundable until May 15. Remaining balance due before camp starts.
What if my kid doesn't like it?
In 87 years, the most common Friday reaction is "can I come back next week?" If your child struggles, staff contacts you immediately — not at end of week.
What about the faith component?
Faith-based but not pushy. Kids experience it through campfire songs, skits, and small groups — not lectures. Many non-religious families love the values-based environment.
Is it safe?
1:5 ratio. Registered health professionals 24/7. OCA accredited since 1997. Vulnerable sector checks on every staff member. 87 years of systems.
What's the difference between Overnight and Day Camp?
Overnight runs Sunday–Friday on Lake Huron — full immersion. Day Camp runs Monday–Friday in London — same adventure, home every night. Both available with the $30 deposit.
